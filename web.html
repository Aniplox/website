Welcome to the Training and Placement Cell of Doon University

The Training & Placement Cell is responsible for facilitating mandatory industrial training for UG and PG students and inviting reputed companies for campus placements. Our goal is to ensure that each student gets the right job opportunities tailored to their skillset and aspirations. We are committed to providing excellent career services to our students, bridging the gap between academia and industry.

Facilitating Industrial Training

Training is mandatory for UG curriculum and is conducted in collaboration with top organizations across the country to ensure students get practical exposure.

Placement for UG and PG Students

We invite top-tier companies across various sectors, including IT, Manufacturing, R&D, and more, to recruit our talented students.

Building Corporate Connections

Strong collaborations with industries and alumni help us connect students to the right opportunities and foster relationships that last.

About the University

Doon University is a premier institute of higher education located in the serene landscapes of Devbhoomi, Uttarakhand. It aims to provide quality education and empower students with skills necessary for their careers. Our state-of-the-art infrastructure and experienced faculty are dedicated to nurturing tomorrow's leaders, focusing on a holistic educational approach that includes both academic and personality development.

Achievements

Doon University has a proven track record of academic excellence, with numerous awards and recognitions in the fields of research, innovation, and student development. Our placement cell has facilitated high-quality internships and job opportunities for students across the country, ensuring a 95% placement rate over the past few years. We take pride in our alumni who have excelled in their respective industries.

Research Capabilities

Our research departments focus on various fields including environmental science, computer science, social studies, and management. We collaborate with various industries and research institutes to foster a culture of innovation and exploration, leading to impactful research outcomes that contribute significantly to society and industry.

Placement Procedure for Companies

Companies interested in recruiting students must fill out the Job Notification Form (JNF) and submit it to our T&P Cell. Slots will be allocated for pre-placement talks, tests, and interviews accordingly. We aim to streamline the process for both companies and students for maximum efficiency and mutual benefit, ensuring that the right talent meets the right opportunity.

Institute Placement Policy

Click the icon to view our Institute Placement Policy. We adhere to transparent and student-friendly placement policies designed to ensure the best outcomes for both students and recruiters.
