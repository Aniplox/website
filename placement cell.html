Career Counselling Training & Placement Cell (CCTP)

About CCTP

The Career Counselling Training & Placement Cell (CCTP) at Doon University is dedicated to enhancing students' employability through various knowledge-based events and activities. The key aspects of CCTP include:

  • Knowledge-based events and activities
  • Collaboration with esteemed firms and institutions
  • Career guidance and counseling
  • Exploration of personal interests, abilities, and aptitudes
  • Professional development training
  • Corporate grooming
